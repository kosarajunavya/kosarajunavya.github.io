LSP is a complete, 1-pill, once-a-day prescription medicine used to treat HIV-1 in adults. LSP does not cure HIV-1 or AIDS. HIV-1 is the virus that causes AIDS.

LSP Goes Where You Go

Wherever your daily life takes you, LSP can go there, too. It's just 1 pill, once a day, taken at the time of day you want, with or without food. When taken every day, LSP can help you get to undetectable, which means the amount of virus in the blood is so low that it canâ€™t be measured by a test (less than 50 copies/mL). According to the U.S. Department of Health and Human Services (DHHS), many patients can get to undetectable in as quickly as 8 to 24 weeks, when taking their treatment as prescribed.
