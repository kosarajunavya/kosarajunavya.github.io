Help them to describe their pain symptoms, including how the pain feels and where on their body they experience it. Encourage them to explain their pain symptoms and severity to their doctor and to join patient support groups. Learn about their pain management plan and help ensure that they stick to medication schedules and dosages. Talk to them about what their physical, emotional and social needs or limitations are, and find out how you can help to improve those areas, so they can maintain a good quality of life. Provide assistance if they need to use walking aids or splints, and arrange to make their living or working environment both safer and more comfortable.
